Pan Con Tomate

116 Calories, 3g Sugar, 178mg Sodium, 5g Fat, 14g Carbs

Originating from Spain, Pan Con Tomate is a local dish dating back to the 16th century. It is a staple in Spanish cooking, and many resturants will ask if you would like to have a side of pan con tomate with your meal.

Ingredients: (Serves 6)

Please beware of any allergies or any other medical afflictions you may have!

  • 2 Ripe Tomatoes
  • 4 Cloves Garlic, Peeled
  • 2 Tablespoons Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 Tablespoons Sherry Vinegar
  • 4-6 Slices Thick-Cut Crusty Bread
  • Flaky Sea Salt to Taste
    •

Instructions: (15 Minutes)

  1. Grate the tomatoes on a box grater. Using a microplane, grate the garlic onto the tomatoes.
  2. Season with kosher salt, the olive oil and the vinegar, and stir lightly to combine.
  3. Toast or grill the bread until it is lightly charred.
  4. Place a heaping tablespoon of the grated tomato mixture onto the bread, then wipe most of it off with the spoon.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil again, add the sea salt, then serve immediately.
