Flan

350 Calories, 57g Carbs, 57g Sugar, 139mg Sodium, 9.5g Protein, 10g Fat

From Spain the Flan is a simple sweet caramel custard dessert. Serve together with fruits or just enjoy it by itself. This delicious beautiful treat will melt in your mouth and leave you wanting more!

Ingredients: (Serves 4)

Please beware of any allergies or any other medical afflictions you may have!

  • 1 Cup White Sugar
  • 4 Eggs
  • 14 oz Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 12 oz Evaporated Milk
  • 1 Tablespoon Vanilla Extract
  • Pinch of Salt


Instructions: (80 Minutes)

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. In a medium saucepan over medium-low heat melt sugar until amber color (be careful to not overcook it). Also, do not stir the pan but do swirl it if needed.
  3. Carefully pour hot syrup into 8 ramekins, turning the dish to evenly coat the bottom and sides.
  4. In a blender add eggs, sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, vanilla and salt. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour egg mixture through a sieve into a measuring cup. Place ramekins onto a baking dish and pour the egg mixture into the ramekins.
  6. Place the baking dish into oven and carefully add hot water up to halfway up the ramekins. Bake for 30-40 minutes.
  7. Carefully remove ramekins from water and let them fully cool. When ready to serve, run and paring knife around the edges and tilt upside down onto a plate. Enjoy!
