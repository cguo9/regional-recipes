Patbingsu (Korean Shaved Ice)

150 Calories, 32g Carbs, 26g Sugar, 36mg Sodium, 3g Protein, 2g Fat

In South Korea, Patbingsu is a popular cool summer dessert to combat the heat. Topped with colorful fruits, red bean and creamy condensed milk, this treat is a refreshing mouthful of sweetness and creaminess! Add your favorite toppings like fruit, jelly or even ice cream to make it even more mouth watering!

Ingredients: (Serves 4)

Please beware of any allergies or any other medical afflictions you may have!

  • 1⁄4 Cup Dried Red Beans or 1⁄2 Cup Sweet Red Beans
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla
  • 1⁄2 Teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • 1 1⁄2 Cups Shaved Ice
  • 2 Tablespoons Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 5-7 Rice Cakes (Optional)
  • 4 Cups Water
  • Fruits
    •

Instructions:

    Make Sweet Red Beans: (70 Minutes)

  1. Rinse and strain 1 cup of red beans and place them in a thick-bottomed pot.
  2. Add 4 cups of water. Cover the pot and bring to a boil over low heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Lower the heat to simmer for 1 hour
  4. Open the lid and check if the beans are cooked well or not. The beans should be crushed easily. Chew a sample of the beans. If there is something hard when you chew, you need to cook longer.
  5. Drain the water and add 1 cup of sugar, 1 ts vanilla, and Â½ ts kosher salt. Mix well.
  6. Uncover and stir the sweet beans over low heat for 5 minutes.
  7. Turn the heat off and let it cool down. Keep in the fridge.

    Make Patbingsu: (10 Minutes)

  1. Add 1⁄2 cup of sweet red beans on the bottom of a dessert bowl.
  2. Add 1 1⁄2 cups of shaved ice over the sweet red beans.
  3. Add all your fruits and desired toppings on top of the ice.
  4. Pour 2 tbs of sweetened condensed milk onto red beans and place 5-7 chopped rice cakes on top if you want.
  5. Eat up before the ice melts!
