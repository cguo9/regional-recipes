Fairy Bread

146 Calories, 5.6g Sugar, 237mg Sodium, 7.7g Fat, 17g Carbs, 2.1g Protien

Originating from Australia, Fairy bread is a local dish usually served at children's parties. The dish can be both a breakfast and a dessert. It is easy to make consisting of just three ingredients, including a ton of sprinkles to add flavor and color to your meal!

Ingredients: (Serves 2)

Please beware of any allergies or any other medical afflictions you may have!

  • 8 Slices White Bread, Crusts Trimmed
  • 1⁄4 Cup Margarine, Softened
  • 1⁄4 Cup Multicolored Candy Sprinkles
  • Optional: Different Shaped Sprinkles
  • Optional: Syrup
    •


Instructions: (10 Minutes)

  1. Spread margarine onto slices of bread.
  2. Cover with sprinkles.
  3. Cut into triangles or horizontally to serve.
