Fish and Chips

450 Calories, 0.2g Sugar, 145mg Sodium, 51g Carbs, 48g Protien

Originating from Britain Australia has made Fish and Chips a staple dish of thier own country. Australian fish and chips offers a wider assortment of fish compared to their British counterpart.

Ingredients: (Serves 4)

Please beware of any allergies or any other medical afflictions you may have!

  • 4 fish fillets (any kind)
  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 1 cup of beer or soda water
  • 3 cups oil for frying
  • 1 teaspoon of salt and pepper
    •

Instructions: (11 Minutes)

  1. Dust fish fillets with cornflour
  2. Mix flour, salt and pepper
  3. Add beer/soda water gradually, stirring well until smooth
  4. Dip the fish fillets into batter and fry until golden brown
  5. Drain on a paper towel and serve
